The problem

The FOIA process
is broken.

01 —
Deadlines slip through the cracks

Twenty business days. That's federal law. Most agencies ignore it. Most journalists never notice until the story is cold.

02 —

Email threads. Spreadsheets. Sticky notes. No journalist has a single view of every request they've filed.

03 —

A thousand pages arrive. No index. Heavy redactions. The story is buried and your deadline is tomorrow.
